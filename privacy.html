Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

Overview

HitTheRoad ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what you can do about it. We collect the minimum needed to make the app work and never sell your data.

What we collect

  • Account data: email address and a unique user ID, used to authenticate you and associate your trips with your account.
  • Profile data (optional): name and profile photo if you choose to add them.
  • Trip data: the trips you generate (start and end points, dates, preferences, generated routes and stops). Stored so you can revisit them.
  • Purchase data: records of in-app credit purchases (product, transaction id, timestamp), used to grant credits to your account.
  • Diagnostic data: anonymous crash reports and error logs via Sentry to help us fix bugs. No personal content is included.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect or store your real-time location.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, microphone, or camera content beyond a profile picture you explicitly upload.
  • We do not run advertising trackers or share data with advertisers.
  • We do not sell your data, ever.

How your data is used

  • To authenticate you and keep your trips associated with your account.
  • To generate trip plans using AI providers (see below). Your prompt is sent to the AI provider only to produce that trip and is not retained for training by us.
  • To process in-app purchases and grant the corresponding credits.
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and errors.

Service providers

  • Supabase (authentication, database, file storage)
  • OpenAI (AI trip generation)
  • Google Maps Platform (place data and routing)
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchase management)
  • Apple (payments, Sign in with Apple)
  • Google (Sign in with Google)
  • Sentry (error monitoring)

Each provider processes data under their own privacy policy. We share with them only what's needed for the feature they power.

Your rights

  • Access: request a copy of your data by emailing us.
  • Delete: delete your account and all data directly in the app at Profile → Account → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and permanent.
  • Correct or update: edit your profile in the app, or email us for anything else.

Children

HitTheRoad is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in the app. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.
