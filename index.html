About

HitTheRoad is an AI-powered road trip planner. Tell us where you're starting, where you're going, and what you love — we build a complete, personalised itinerary in under a minute, with optimised routes, hidden gems, day-by-day breakdowns, and estimated fuel costs.

Each trip generation uses one credit. New accounts start with a few free credits and additional credits can be purchased in-app (non-renewing, no subscription).
